A SaaS layer orchestrating secure, audited access to existing regulated financial services without assuming balance sheet or custody risk.

1. Integration, Not Replacement

We do not replace Core Banking. We orchestrate access while preserving existing legacy systems.

2. On-Chain Orchestration, Off-Chain Execution

Blockchain handles permissions and logs. Heavy financial logic and sensitive data remain off-chain.

Data minimization. Personal data never written to the public ledger — only validity proofs (ZKP/Hashes).

Built from day one for GDPR, eIDAS 2.0, DORA, MiFID II. [Legal analysis: Audens, March 2026 — subject to final product design decisions]
